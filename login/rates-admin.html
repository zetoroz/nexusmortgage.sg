How to update rates:
  1. Replace /login/rates.xlsx with your new sheet.
  2. From the project root, run python3 scripts/convert-rates.py.
  3. Refresh this page — new rates show under "Current Rates", and a new entry appears in "Change History" with what changed and by how much.
  4. Commit and push login/rates.xlsx, rates.json, and rates-history.json.
