2. Scope of services

Nexus is an independent mortgage brokerage operating in Singapore. Our services include:

  • Comparing residential home loan packages across our partner banks (currently 16 banks).
  • Producing written advisory documents (e.g., the Free Refinancing Report) on request.
  • Submitting your loan application to the bank you select, with your consent.
  • Coordinating with bank representatives, valuers, and conveyancing lawyers as required.
  • Post-disbursement check-ins around lock-in expiry, where you have opted in.

We do not act as a lender. Loan approval, disbursement, and final terms are issued solely by the bank you select.

3. Fees & broker compensation

For residential clients in Singapore, our advisory service is offered at zero broker fee. Banks pay us a referral commission upon successful loan disbursement. The commission paid to us does not affect the rate or package available to you — bank packages are quoted directly by the bank.

Engagements outside the residential-mortgage scope (for example, certain commercial property, offshore property, or specialist structuring work) may be subject to a separate written fee arrangement, which will be agreed with you in advance.

  • Change without notice by the issuing bank.

  • Bank credit assessment and final Letter of Offer terms.

Indicative rates do not constitute an offer of credit.

Decisions on additional buyer's stamp duty (ABSD), seller's stamp duty (SSD), decoupling, trust structures, or estate planning should be confirmed with an appropriately qualified professional before completion.
