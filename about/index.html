Our Story

Why We Started Nexus

Most home buyers in Singapore go to the bank they already use. It feels safe, but it almost always costs them. The package on the shelf is rarely the best one for the borrower — it's the one the bank wants to push that quarter.

Nexus was set up to fix that. We sit on the borrower's side of the table and run a full sixteen-bank comparison every time, fixed and SORA-pegged, with the same information the relationship manager has. The bank pays us for the introduction; you pay nothing.

That model only works if we stay genuinely independent. So we don't take retainers from any single bank, we don't push promo packages that don't suit you, and we tell you when the right answer is to do nothing. Refinancing six months early to chase a 0.05% rate cut isn't advice — it's churn.

Our Principles

Five Things We Don't Compromise On

These aren't slogans — they're the operating rules that decide what advice we give and which engagements we walk away from.

01

Full-market comparison

Every recommendation comes from the same sixteen-bank scan. No hidden shortlist, no preferred-partner package promoted ahead of a better one.

02

Zero fee to the borrower

The bank pays us a referral fee only on successful placement. You see exactly the same package terms a relationship manager would offer — at no cost to you.

03

Honest "do nothing" advice

If your current package is already optimal, we tell you. We don't manufacture refinancing churn for a fee, and we don't push cash-out for clients who don't need it.

04

MAS-rule transparency

TDSR at 4% stress, MSR at 30%, LTV by tenure and age — every calculation we do is grounded in the published MAS rules. We show our working.

05

Lifetime relationship

The first loan is rarely the last. We diary your lock-in expiry, watch the rate cycle for you, and surface refinance windows when they actually move the needle.

06

No data leakage

Income statements and CPF disclosures stay between you, us, and the bank you choose. We don't sell leads, run remarketing pixels on your data, or cross-promote unrelated products.

What to Expect

Working With Nexus

We're a small advisory by design — every engagement runs through a senior advisor, not a call-centre script. That keeps the advice consistent and the timeline tight.

  • 15-minute discovery call, then a written rate comparison within 24 hours.
  • You see the actual three best packages — not a pre-curated "winner".
  • End-to-end handling — we chase the bank, you don't.
