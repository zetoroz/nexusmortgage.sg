Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Your maximum loan depends on TDSR (Total Debt Servicing Ratio), capped at 55% of gross income under the MAS stress test at 4% p.a. For HDB flats, an additional MSR cap of 30% applies. Use our Affordability Calculator or WhatsApp us for a free personalised assessment.

Fixed rate home loans lock in an interest rate for 1–3 years, giving certainty on monthly payments. SORA-linked (floating) loans track the Singapore Overnight Rate Average — currently around 1.02% (3M Compounded SORA) — plus a bank spread. SORA packages often start lower but can rise. Nexus compares both to find the best fit for your situation.

The best time to refinance is when your lock-in period ends (typically 2–3 years after taking the loan). Switching just 0.3% lower on a S$800,000 loan saves roughly S$2,400 per year. Nexus provides a free refinance savings analysis — contact us to find out how much you can save.

Our service is 100% free to borrowers. Banks pay us a referral fee when a loan is successfully placed. You receive independent advice, a full market comparison, and full application support at zero cost.

TDSR (Total Debt Servicing Ratio) is a MAS rule that limits total monthly debt repayments (including the new mortgage, stress-tested at 4% p.a.) to 55% of gross income. For HDB flats, MSR (Mortgage Servicing Ratio) further limits the home loan portion to 30% of income. Our free Affordability Check calculates your exact position.

Credit card balances quietly cut your borrowing power. Under MAS rules, lenders treat 5% of your total outstanding card balances — or S$50 per card, whichever is higher — as a monthly debt obligation in your TDSR. A S$30,000 balance silently subtracts S$1,500/month of TDSR room, roughly S$300k of borrowing capacity at 30-year tenure. Pay down balances and consolidate cards before applying — try our Affordability Check with your real card numbers to see the impact.

In-principle approval (IPA) typically takes 3–5 business days once documents are submitted. Full formal loan approval follows within 1–2 weeks. Nexus handles all bank submissions and follows up on your behalf to keep the process fast.
